THE WORKSHOP

Mold. Pour.
Cure. Release.

Every piece starts as a mold I build by hand. The concrete is mixed, poured, and left to cure for days — it can't be rushed. When it's ready, I break the mold away. What's left keeps every mark of how it was made. That's the point.

01
Mold
Built by hand for each form.
02
Pour
Mixed and cast in small batches.
03
Cure
Days, not hours. It sets on its own time.
04
Release
The mold breaks away. The marks stay.

THE MAKER

Tom Beton

I make brutalist objects in concrete. Bauhaus and minimalism are where I started, and I never really left. I'm interested in the honest version of a thing — the material as it is, the joint left visible, the weight in your hand. Everything here is made by me, one at a time.
